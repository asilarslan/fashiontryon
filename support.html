üìã Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What makes a good photo for try-on?
A: For best results, use photos that are:
  • Well-lit with good lighting
  • Full-body shots showing head to toe
  • Taken against a plain background
  • High resolution and not blurry
  • Showing you facing the camera directly
Q: How long does AI processing take?
A: AI processing typically takes 30-60 seconds depending on image complexity and server load. You'll see a progress indicator while your try-on is being generated.
Q: Can I use photos of other people?
A: You should only upload photos of yourself or photos where you have explicit permission from the person. Uploading photos without consent violates our Terms of Use.
Q: Are my photos stored on your servers?
A: Photos are processed temporarily for AI generation and then deleted from our servers. Generated results are stored locally on your device. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
Q: What's included in the premium version?
A: Premium features include:
  • Unlimited try-on sessions
  • Access to exclusive clothing collections
  • Higher quality AI processing
  • Priority processing speed
  • Advanced editing tools
Q: Why isn't my try-on result accurate?
A: AI results depend on photo quality and clothing compatibility. Try using:
  • Better lighting in your photo
  • A clearer, higher resolution image
  • Different clothing items
  • A more direct camera angle

üí° Tips for Best Results

üì∏ Photo Tips:

  • Lighting: Use natural light or bright indoor lighting
  • Background: Plain, solid-colored backgrounds work best
  • Pose: Stand straight, arms slightly away from body

  • Clothing: Wear fitted clothes for better AI recognition

üëó Clothing Selection Tips:
