Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Welcome to Fashion Try-On! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our AI-powered virtual fashion try-on application.

1. Information We Collect

Photos and Images

  • Full-body photos you upload for virtual try-on experiences
  • Clothing images you select or upload
  • Generated try-on results created by our AI

Usage Information

  • App usage patterns and features used
  • Device information (iOS version, device model)
  • Crash reports and performance data

2. How We Use Your Information

  • AI Processing: Your photos are processed by our AI to create virtual try-on results
  • App Functionality: To provide and improve our fashion try-on services
  • Quality Improvement: To enhance AI accuracy and user experience
  • Support: To provide customer support when needed

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Photos are processed securely and temporarily stored for AI processing
  • Generated results are stored locally on your device
  • We use industry-standard encryption and security measures
  • Data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal photos with third parties, except:

  • AI Processing: Photos are sent to our secure AI processing servers (FAL AI)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or legal process
  • Safety: To protect the rights and safety of users

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your photos and data
  • Opt-out: Stop using the app at any time
  • Local Storage: Generated images are stored locally and can be deleted by you

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

7. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • FAL AI: For AI-powered fashion try-on processing
  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and in-app purchases

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information during such transfers.

9. Data Retention

  • Uploaded photos are processed and then deleted from our servers
  • Generated results are stored locally on your device
  • Usage data may be retained for app improvement purposes

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes through the app or by email.
